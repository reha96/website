# Sanzo Wada's color dictionary, online and LLM-friendly

While [rebuilding my personal website last week](https://www.rehatuncer.com/blog/2026/06/23/ai-powered-personal-website-rebuild),
finding a new color scheme that "felt" like me was top priority. My partner had bought
a paper copy of _A Dictionary of Color Combinations_ by Sanzo Wada and with all the
color pairings it contains, it seemed perfect for picking a new dark and light palette.

Describing colors to an LLM — Oil Green, Coral Red, Light Glaucous Blue, Sulphur
Yellow — feels weird. Appreciation of color is so subjective. But with a reference to
their origin (Sanzo Wada), any LLM assistant can find the specifics. What's even
better is the [online version](https://colors.elwyn.co/) of the book, showing 100+
two-color combinations with hex codes and historical color names. I found multiple
versions of the book online; some give heavy vibe-coded vibes.
